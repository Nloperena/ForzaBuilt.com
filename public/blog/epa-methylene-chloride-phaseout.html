EPA Is Phasing Out Methylene Chloride in Industrial Adhesives: What You Need to Know and Do

Understanding the EPA's Methylene Chloride Phase-Out Methylene chloride, a powerful solvent commonly used in industrial adhesives and other applications, is being phased out by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) due to its significant health risks.

Staying compliant with industry regulations is essential for any manufacturing operation. This guide covers the latest regulatory changes and their impact on adhesive applications.
